“The Distribution of Power in Exchange Networks: Theory and Experimental Results”Review

https://www.jstor.org/stable/2779142

Emerson(1972) 교환 네트워크의 정의:

“(1) a set of actors (either natural persons or corporate groups), (2) a distribution of valued resources among those actors, (3) for each actor a set of exchange opportunities with other actors in the network, (4) a set of historically developed and utilized exchange opportunities called exchange relations,2 and (5) a set of network connections linking exchange relations into a single network structure. Thus an "exchange network" is a specific social structure formed by two or more connected exchange relations between actors” (Cook 等, 1983, p. 277) (1) 一组参与者（自然人或企业集团），(2) 在这些参与者之间分配有价值的资源，(3) 为每个参与者提供一组与网络中其他参与者的交流机会，(4)一组历史上开发和利用的j交换机会，称为交换关系 (5) 一组将交换关系链接成​​单一网络结构的网络连接。因此，“交换网络”是由两个或多个参与者之间相互联系的交换关系形成的特定社会结构。

Connection의 정의 [ positive  or negative ]

  • “Definition 1: Two exchange relations between actors A-B and actors A-C are connected to form the minimal network B-A-C to the degree that exchange in one relation is contingent on exchange (or nonexchange) in the other relation.

    • (a) The connection is positive if exchange in one relation is contingent on exchange in the other.
    • (b) The connection is negative if exchange in one relation is contingent on nonexchange in the other.” (Cook 等, 1983, p. 277) 定义 1：参与者 A-B 和参与者 A-C 之间的两个交换关系连接形成最小网络 B-A-C，其程度是一种关系中的交换取决于另一种关系中的交换（或非交换）。 (a) 如果一种关系中的交换取决于另一种关系中的交换，则这种联系是积极的。 (b) 如果一种关系中的交换取决于另一种关系中的不交换，则这种联系是负的。

3방(3方) 연결 필여 없이  “If the A-B and A-C exchange relations are connected (by definition 1), they define the minimal network or component, B-A-C”

  • B,C가 A의 가대체 대상 -> 경쟁 -> Negative relation
  • A가 B,C의 대리 -> 의존 -> Positive relation

대체관계의 존재 -> 환합 구조 Relation network의 경우 더 많음

Position in an Exchange Network

  • Vote -> Actor
  • Edge -> Relation

“Definition 2: A position in a graph or network is a set of one or more points whose residual graphs are isomorphic.” (Cook 等, 1983, p. 279) 定义 2：图或网络中的位置是一组一个或多个点，其残差图是同构的。

fg1. all figure in 1 is negative relation

“In the particular structures diagramed in figure 1, solid lines represent more profitable exchange opportunities than broken lines (by a factor of three to one);” (Cook 等, 1983, p. 280) 在图 1 所示的特定结构中，实线表示比虚线更有利可图的交易机会（三比一）；

  • all figure in 1 is negative relation

    • “. These emergent networks are negatively connected. If an actor has two solid-line opportunities, the two partners represented are fully interchangeable as sources of benefit. With finite exchange time available, any use of one opportunity means that another opportunity is forgone.” (Cook 等, 1983, p. 280) .这些新兴网络是负连接的。如果一个演员有两个实线机会，那么所代表的两个合作伙伴作为利益来源是完全可以互换的。在有限的交换时间可用的情况下，任何一个机会的使用都意味着另一个机会被放弃。

이 특징으로 권력의 분배가 기회 위치 구조의 함수로 연구

“The results concerning power use demonstrated that power is a function of position in the network, even when the position occupants are ignorant of the actual network structure and their own position in it” (Cook 等, 1983, p. 281) 关于权力使用的结果表明，权力是网络中位置的函数，即使位置占用者不了解实际的网络结构和他们自己在其中的位置

권력의 예측 in negatively connected network : 단순 네트워크 구조 중심성 or 권력 의존성 ??

In negative relation network?

“In our negatively connected networks, however, the flow of resources is between two adjacent actors (i.e., points) with no intermediary; that is, there is a direct as opposed to an indirect exchange of resources.” (Cook 等, 1983, p. 284) 然而，在我们的负连接网络中，资源的流动是在两个相邻的参与者（即点）之间，没有中间人；也就是说，有直接而不是间接的资源交换。

negative relation -> 직접 교환 관계 ((중계)간접관계 없음) -> 어떤 Centrality algorithm에 적용할 수 없는 듯이다.

네트워크의 유형에 따라 권력분석의 중심성 알고리즘의 선정 기준 밝혀야 한다.

Emerson의 권력 의존 이론

  • “Definition 3: In any dyadic exchange relation Ax; B, (where A and B are actors, and x and y are resources introduced in exchange), the power of A over B (P_AB) is the potential of A to obtain favorable outcomes at B's expense.” (Cook 等, 1983, p. 284) 定义 3：在任何二元交换关系 Ax 中； B，（其中 A 和 B 是参与者，x 和 y 是作为交换引入的资源），A 对 B 的权力（P_AB）是 B从A 获得有利结果的潜力。
  • “Definition 4: The dependence (D_AB) of A on B in a dyadic exchange relation(e.g., Ax; BO) is a joint function (1) varying directly with the value of y to A, and (2) varying inversely with the availability of y to A from alternate sources.” (Cook 等, 1983, p. 285) A对B的依赖指：（1）直接随 y 到 A 的值变化，以及（2）随 y 到 A 从其他来源的可用性成反比变化。
  • P_AB == D_BA

교환 자원의 중요성 있고 같다는 환경에서:

중심성 알고리즘에 의해 D와 E 같은 권력을 가지고 있지만

  • D(FE) > D(DE) -> P(EF) > P(ED) ->E의 자원 비중 F에게 편향 -> 경쟁(D,F) -> P(DE)감소

  • 따라서 P(E) > [P(D) == P(F)]

교환 자원 중요성 낮다 -> 의뢰의 감소, 불평등의 감소

교환 자원의 분배 -> 탐육적 분배 -> 어떤 평균의 달성

“Considering the structures in figure 1, if solid lines represent 24 units of profit to be divided through exchange and dashed lines represent only eight units of negotiable profit, then the best alternative source for all occupants of position F is fixed at four points (8/2). Thus, if power were exerted to its absolute maximum in these networks, all occupants of position E would obtain 20 points per exchange, while all occupants of positions F and D would obtain four points per exchange when equilibrium is reached (i.e., when the exchange ratios have stabilized). If there are restraints on the exercise of power (e.g., equity concerns or less than fully rational negotiation), equilibrium will be reached somewhere short of this maximally "exploitive" exchange ratio (see Cook and Emerson 1978).” (Cook 等, 1983, p. 287)

가치 자원의 상대 가용성-> 가 대체(可代替)성 -> 의뢰성 -> 권력

“E's use of power over D will emerge more slowly in network ld than in network 1c.” (Cook 等, 1983, p. 288) E 在网络 ld 中对 D 的使用将比在网络 1c 中出现得更慢。

D의 연결 수 E보다 많아서 자원 우세 가지고 있다.

1(c)보다 D의 권력 감소 속도 느린다.

“E's use of power over F will emerge more quickly in network if than in le, where it will emerge more quickly than in id” (Cook 等, 1983, p. 288) E 对 F 的权力使用在网络 if 中比在 le 中出现得更快，在 le 中它比在 id 中出现得更快

1(f)의P(EF) > 1(e)의 P(EF)
